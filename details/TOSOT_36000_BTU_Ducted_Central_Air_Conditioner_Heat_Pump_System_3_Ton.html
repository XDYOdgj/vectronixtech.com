Efficient Climate Control for Every Season

The TOSOT 36000 BTU ducted central air conditioner heat pump system offers reliable and efficient heating and cooling for your home, no matter the weather. Whether it's the intense heat of Arizona or the cold snap of Michigan, this system ensures your space stays comfortable year-round. Its advanced two-stage compressor delivers consistent performance and enhances the longevity of the unit, making it a smart choice for homeowners seeking durability and efficiency.

With Energy Star certification, this model is designed to save energy without sacrificing performance, helping you reduce utility costs and minimize your environmental footprint. The system's high SEER2 and HSPF2 ratings ensure optimal energy efficiency, making it an economical and eco-friendly solution. Its compact design also makes installation easier, especially in limited or confined spaces, without compromising cooling or heating capacity.

Versatile Installation and Enhanced Performance

Engineered for both residential and commercial applications, this TOSOT system comes with an indoor unit that can be mounted horizontally or vertically, providing flexibility for basements, attics, or other awkward areas. Equipped with inverter technology, the system achieves up to 30% greater energy efficiency, making it ideal for homeowners who prioritize energy savings. The included 25-foot copper line set and professional installation support streamline the process, ensuring a seamless and efficient setup. This combination of features offers a powerful, affordable, and eco-conscious way to maintain perfect indoor temperatures for years to come.
