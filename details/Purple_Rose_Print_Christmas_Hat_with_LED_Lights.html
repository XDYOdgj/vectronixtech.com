Festive Purple Rose Print Christmas Hat with LED Lights

This festive hat with a vibrant purple rose print is the perfect blend of style and holiday spirit. Whether you're preparing for a cozy winter gathering or a lively festive event, it's designed to elevate your look with a touch of elegance and cheer. The full-scale print adds a striking visual element, while the plush lining ensures all-day comfort without compromising on style.

With built-in LED lights, this hat provides a warm and festive ambiance, making it ideal for Christmas, New Year, or Halloween celebrations. The LED components can be easily controlled via a button on the battery, offering three lighting modes: fast flash, slow flash, and no flash. This flexibility allows you to customize the mood and brightness that suits your occasion best.

Constructed from durable polyester and flannel, this hat maintains its color vibrancy and texture even after repeated use, making it a reliable choice for seasonal events. Its adjustable fit ensures a secure and comfortable wear for both children and adults, while the easy-to-clean feature makes maintenance simple. Just remove the LED lights and wash by hand or machine to keep it looking shiny and new.
